Projects

Byte Boulevard

Byte boulevard is a comprehensive full-stack e-commerce platform designed to provide users with a seamless experience in exploring a diverse menu featuring a variety of food and beverage options. Users benefit from the convenience of accessing detailed information, including descriptions and pricing, for each item. Additionally, my platform empowers users to effortlessly add their preferred food and beverage selections to their shopping cart. Once added, users can conveniently review and manage their cart, containing a comprehensive list of their purchases. However, for enhanced customization and editing capabilities within the cart, users are required to register for an account.

Spaceaholics

Spaceaholics is a front end ajax application that is designed to implement various NASA api calls including NASA'S daily APOD images, searching features, etc. User has the flexibility of searching and storing their favorite NASA related images to a favorites page. By clicking on the photo, the user can see a closer frame of the image along with its individual description. On the page, there's a pencil icon where the user goes back home when the icon is clicked and is able to edit the existing photo or delete there existing favorited image by clicking on the black hole or returning back to your favorited list by clicking on the sun icon.
